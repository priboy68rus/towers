Towers

In this game you need to capture towers with your soldiers. Here are rules:

  • There are total of 10 towers with values from 1 to 10
  • To capture one you need to send more soldiers than your opponent
  • You have only 50 soldiers so use them wisely
  • Player wins if he or she has the highest sum of captured towers values

To play, enter your nickname and select a room from a list down below or create your own. Have fun :)
